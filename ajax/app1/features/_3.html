Browse & Chirp Users

We validate interest by allowing users to "chirp" one another. A "chirp" is an anonymous match request you can send to a user, in the same way one adds someone as a friend on Facebook, or follows another on Instagram. If a user chirps you back, it's a match!

There are 5 unique ways of chirping users and none of them include swiping:

  • Browse & Chirp through University group pages
  • Browse & Chirp through "Flocks" group pages
  • Search specifically for a crush and chirp
  • Chirp users detected within plain sight (300 feet)
  • Chirp users from phone contacts
