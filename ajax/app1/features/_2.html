"Flocks"

The people you fly with

Flocks are key differentiator in Parrot, that allows users to self-identify in groups with other Parrot users. These "Flocks" can be but aren't limited to fraternities, sororities, social clubs & organizations, living groups, companies, sports teams, etc. We allow users to browse through these Flocks as it is helpful in finding and browsing through users who you know may already know.
